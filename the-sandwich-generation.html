About The Sandwich Generation

The Sandwich Generation is a cross-generational podcast designed to bridge the divide between generations. Its premise is one generation is “sandwiched” between another; e.g. the middle or the bridge between the two. It sets to engage the two “opposing” generations by exploring complex topics—politics, culture, religion, education, technology, and societal change—etc.; to cultivate understanding, reduce generational tension, and elevate the shared significance of each era. At a moment when generational narratives are often polarized, The Sandwich Generation offers a platform for meaningful conversation, perspective-sharing, and common ground.

The Sandwich Generation will be:

- Culturally aware

- Curious

- Honest

- Bridge-building

- Our conversational, not preachy

- Thought-provoking, Open-minded and grounded

We will be:

- Respectful of differences

- Not to be forgotten - A dash of light humor, creativity and fun!

Our Themes will be:

- Cross-generational

- Shared stories and lived experience

- Culture & change: where we agree, disagree, and grow

- Respecting the old while embracing the new

- How society shifts—and who shapes it

- Everyday life through various lenses
